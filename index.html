Somethings in life

are better written than said
so that the souls always remembers
so that the heart always ponders
so that the mind always pictures

You wrote in my heart once

I just want to be even

but who says that we must be?

What if we can never be even

and I write pages on end in your life
and you do the same?

The beautiful stories

the sad ones
the happy ones

Take the pen and continue,
they are always memorable

HAPPY VALENTINES!!

From the Sensei
